Welcome to my photography portfolio

I'm a Brazilian who got a lot of interest in the Web Design field. After working as a graphic designer in some ad agencies and as a marketing analyst in a company in Brazil, I decided to move to the U.S. to study more about Web Design and just simply fell in love with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. In my free time, I love to travel and take pictures. This website is a small portfolio of some pictures I took of family and friends during the last few years. Feel free to explore this website and email me if you have questions!

Places and People.

I've been living in three different countries until now, and this allowed me to meet people from other places on earth with the most distinct background and ethnicity. I believe that the experience of living abroad showed me how similar and how dissimilar humankind can be. But that there are more things that can bring us together than things that can bring us apart.

Travel and Explore.

I was always interested in other cultures and countries besides the one I was born in (Brazil). Since my teenage years, I was searching for new places to visit one day and discovering new things around the world that would be fascinating to see in person. I can say that I'm lucky enough to be able to see some of these places I've dreamed of seeing in the 12 countries I visit so far. But there are way more places to see and visit.
